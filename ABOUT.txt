Introducing Garbage Grab!

Step into the neon-drenched, dystopian future of Garbage Grab, 
where humanity has achieved interstellar travel, bridging worlds and cultures. 
As humans explore distant planets, alien species, including the Glub, seek refuge on Earth after their homeworld’s destruction. 
With their eerie, demon-like appearance, the Glub are shunned by society and relegated to the fringes—namely, humanity’s sprawling, high-tech garbage dumps. 
These automated sorting facilities recycle or destroy waste, but amidst the chaos, valuable discarded tech often slips through.

Enter the Garbage Grabbers, daring pilots who navigate these hazardous zones in advanced mech suits, scavenging high-value items before they’re lost forever. 
They face fierce competition from rival Grabbers and the territorial Glub, who roam the dumps, resentful of intruders. 

In a world where humanity generates more waste than ever, "grabbing" has become a lucrative, high-stakes profession.

Game Overview

Garbage Grab is a thrilling 3D multiplayer game set in a gritty cyberpunk arena—a high-tech garbage dump shrouded in fog, dotted with junk piles and spike traps. 
Players control customizable mechs, battling AI opponents and evading a menacing Glub monster, 
all while collecting valuable loot: Gold Coins (10 points), Blue USB Sticks (25 points), and Silver Laptops (50 points). The objective is simple yet intense: achieve the highest score within a 5-minute timer, dodging hazards and outsmarting rivals to claim victory. The game features a dynamic HUD displaying health, timer, score, and a leaderboard, with immersive audio (cyberpunk music and sound effects) and visual effects (bloom, vignette, particles, screen shake).

Gameplay and Controls
Playable on Desktop and Mobile: Enjoy seamless gameplay across platforms.
Desktop: Use W, A, S, D keys for movement and rotation (tank-style), with the Space bar to jump.

Mobile: A virtual joystick provides fluid 360-degree movement, automatically adjusting the mech’s direction.

Characters: Pilot your unique Mech-D5Ww2Jdo42, compete against AI TrashBots (TrashBot_1 and TrashBot_2), or other players, 
and avoid the Glub Evolved monster, which chases and damages players within its radius.

Arena: Navigate a grid-based floor with fog, junk piles as obstacles, and spike traps that deal damage on contact.

Goals: Collect the most points by grabbing loot, survive the 5-minute round (or until health depletes), and aim for the top spot on the leaderboard.

Blockchain and Gorbagana Chain Integration

Garbage Grab is powered by the innovative Gorbagana Chain, a fast, Solana-based experimental testnet designed for seamless blockchain integration. 
The game includes placeholders for future blockchain features, such as a "Connect Wallet" button that simulates wallet connection (logging "Wallet connected" and updating the player name to "Ghxst.eth" on the leaderboard). 

The Gorbagana Chain enables a competitive edge with an on-chain leaderboard, where scores are recorded using native test tokens ($gGOR). Top players can earn $gGOR prizes, fostering a vibrant, decentralized gaming ecosystem. 

This integration is still in development, with plans to fully support Backpack wallet and enhance multiplayer functionality on the testnet and mainnet when live.

Development and Accessibility

Built using a modern, buildless JavaScript stack—Three.js for 3D rendering, React Three Fiber and Drei for React integration, React Three Rapier for physics, Zustand for state management, 
and Howler.js for audio—Garbage Grab is designed for simplicity and performance. 
The code is hosted on this GitHub repository, exported from Rosebud AI, and includes all assets (3D models, textures, audio) in a downloadable zip.

Whether you’re a developer looking to contribute or a player eager to explore, the game is open for testing and improvement.
